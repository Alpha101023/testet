About Us

Band Introduction

  • Our band was founded in 2008 by three close friends: Jay, our talented bassist; Valerie, our dynamic drummer; and Harris, who leads the charge on guitar. What started as a shared passion for music quickly grew into a collaborative journey of creativity and performance. A few years ago, we were thrilled to expand our family by welcoming four incredibly talented members: Joice and Emerson, who bring powerful vocals and vibrant energy to our performances; Justin, whose rhythm guitar adds depth and texture to our sound; and Albert, whose skill on the keyboard elevates our compositions. Together, we’ve embraced the opportunity to evolve and craft music that reflects the diversity of our styles and influences. Our music is a fusion of rock, pop, reggae, and hip-hop, and we love experimenting with different genres to create a unique sound that connects with our audience. Every performance is a reflection of our collective passion and dedication, and we can’t wait to share that with you.
